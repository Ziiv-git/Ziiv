Zomato Rating Prediction

  • Perform extensive Exploratory Data Analysis(EDA) on the Zomato Dataset which will be described as below as you scroll down.

  • Build an appropriate Machine Learning Model that will help various Zomato Restaurants to predict their respective Ratings based on certain features.

  • Deploy the Machine learning model via Flask that can be used to make live predictions of restaurants ratings.

Important note: Data is hosted on kaggle and its not a new data, so do keep in mind. Below are the key insights.

  • Python, Google Colab notebook, flask, scikit-learn libraries were used.

Famous Restuarants in Bangalore

Favourite food of Bangalore

  • 5 most fav food of Banglorean that which are ordered or had outside at restaurants are pasta, pizza, cocktails, burgers and mocktails.

Type of restaurants preferred

  • Casual dining, quick bites and cafe are 3 most common types of restaurants in Bangalore.

Number of restaurants providing online bookings

  • 0 = No facility for online booking
  • 1 = Online booking available

Most of the ratings for restaurants were between 3.5 to 4.5

Two most common services are dine out and delivery.

Cost of food for two people

  • Min cost for ordering food is 40 ruppees.
  • Average price is around 600 ruppees for cost of food for two people.
  • From this figure we cannot say that as price increase the quality of food increases.
