Flight Price Prediction

  • Curated data using firstly with automated analysing and based on that manually cleaned the data.
  • Studied data through analysing various features present in data and tried to collect what insights could be taken.
  • Performance measure was r2_score, trid various models but Catboost regressor performed better than rest.
  • Python, Google Colab notebook, flask, Heroku, scikit-learn libraries were used.

Analysis of dataset.

  • Jet Airways Business flights are the costliest, rest are in neck to neck competition when it comes to prices.
  • Vistara Premium economy tops the chart when it comes to pricing based on stops a flight takes in a jaourney, it is costliest in one and two stop flights.
  • Air India is the only flight that gives 4 stoppage flight.
  • Considering non-stop and one stop flight between various carriers, Indigo, Air India, GoAir and Air Asia are in fight.
  • Based on Sources, boarding from Chennai and Mumbai would do good for the travellers.
  • Its a correlation matrix show the relations between two features, also it does imply causation i.e. one does not occur due to others.
  • The middle white diagonal is the correlation of feature with itself.
  • The dark blue squares either denotes positive or negative correlation between features. ‘-’ represents the negative correlation and vice versa.
  • Closer the number to either -1 or 1, the more the correlation between features.
  • Total stops and Price has a high negative correlation with Indigo, Source_Kolkata having high negative correlation with Source_Delhi and so on.
  • This matrix is used to see and check for multi-collinearity, which in our dataset there are not many such features high collinearity.
