HR Analytics

Objective:

  • Identifying, classifying and quantifying the productivity of emplopyees.
  • Calculate ROI of emplopyee initiatives
  • Helps in supporting the business decisions with the backing of data.

Tools used: Excel

HR Metrics:

  • That helps organization track their human capital and measure how effective their human resources initiatives are.

  • Staffing metrics:

    • Time to start: average number of days taken to fill the vacant position.
    • Time to productivity: average number of days taken to satisfy productivity.
    • Accession rate: rate at which employees are joining the organization in a given time period.
    • Turn over rate: rate at which employees are leaving the firm in a given time period.
    • Cost per external/internal hires: average cost incurred while hiring.
    • other metrics: offer rate, offer decline rate, promotion rate, retention, vacancy rate, vacancy costs, retirement risks

  • Training and Development metrics:

    • Competency rate: Degree to which employees in key positions have the competencies necessary to achieve the performance objective.
    • Readiness: reflects how ready the firm is from human capital perspective to execute on strategy and achieve key goals and objectives.
    • Training spend rate: relative importance of spending on Training vs other operating and human capital activities.
    • Training head count investment: amount of investment in training for each employee.

  • Performance metrics:

    • performance review completion rate: % of completed reviews.
    • Average performance rating: mean performance rating across a selected group of employees receiving performance assessments.
    • Performance rating distribution: employee representation across each of available performance ratings.
