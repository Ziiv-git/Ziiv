Sales Insights

Project done while learning with the help from codebasics

Problem statement:

  • A company which supplies hardware peripherals to different clients have head office at Delhi and many regional offices in different states of India. Their issue is their sales are declining and they want to know various insights about why this is happening and also see if any strategies can be devised to improve the sales.

Key things learnt in this project:

  • Aims Grid

    • Purpose

      • To unlock sales insights that are not visible before sales team for decision support and automate them to reduce manual time spent in data gathering.

    • Stakeholders

      • People/Staff involved in this project.

    • End result

      • An automated dashboard providing quick and latest sales insights in order to support data driven decision making.

    • Success criteria

      • Dashboard uncovering sales order insights with latest data available.
      • Sales team able to take better decisions and improve 10% cost savings of total spend.
      • Sales analysts to stop gathering data manually in order to save 20% of their business time and reinvest in value added activity.

  • Data Exploration

    • Total revenue and profit generated per year
    • Revenue by markets
    • Sales quantity by markets
    • Top 5 customers and products
    • Profit margin by markets
    • Profit trend
    • pie chart of customer types

  • Querying using SQL

    • No of records in table
    • seeing the Chennai transactions data
    • seeing the rows where currency is in USD
    • transactions data based on year 2020
    • Total sum of sales in year 2020
    • total sales amount in year 2020 of Chennai region
    • unique products sold in Chennai region
