ABOUT ME

It’s Devyang, entry-level Data science professional, passionate about studying the factors that drive performance starting from a brain of child to top of the business chain. After having prepared for UPSC for four years, then decided to transition into field of Data and Data Science. When starting out towards this journey I was completely lost as with UPSC in the first year; there were loads of resources available but had very little guidance on how about to initiate my engine. One fine day, I decided just to start out with coding courses and from there on as knowledge came, started deep diving into journey with more interests and enthusiasm, because at every step it gets exciting in this field.

I love to write code, it’s my passion. I am here to help you, I can help you to describe data and its trends and insights in the best way. No matter how difficult, I will find an easy way and complete your work anyway. Stay connected with me……..😊
